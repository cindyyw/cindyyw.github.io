About me

I received my Ph.D. from Beihang University advised by Prof. Xudong Liu and Prof. Hailong Sun.
I worked with Prof. Shan Lu in University of Chicago in 2016, as a visiting student.
I received my bachelor's degree in computer science and technology from Jilin University, in 2012.
My research interests include software reliability, distribtued systems, and intelligent software engineering.
